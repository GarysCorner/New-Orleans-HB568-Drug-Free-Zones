### Digest of HB 568

[Full info on HB568](https://www.legis.la.gov/legis/BillInfo.aspx?s=26rs&b=HB568)

The digest printed below was prepared by House Legislative Services. It constitutes no part of the
legislative instrument. The keyword, one-liner, abstract, and digest do not constitute part of the law
or proof or indicia of legislative intent. [R.S. 1:13(B) and 24:177(E)]
HB 568 Reengrossed 2026 Regular Session Firment
Abstract: Enhances the penalty for violating the Uniform Controlled Dangerous Substances Law
with respect to marijuana within 2,000 feet of a school zone.
Present law provides that a person who violates certain provisions of the Uniform Controlled
Dangerous Substances Law (UCDS) within 2,000 feet of a school zone shall be punished by the
maximum fine and one and one-half times the longest term of imprisonment provided in the UCDS.
Proposed law provides that a person who violates the provisions of the UCDS with respect to
marijuana shall be imprisoned with or without hard labor for not more than one year and may be
fined up to $1,000.
(Amends R.S. 40:981.3(D); Adds R.S. 40:981.3(A)(4) and (E))
